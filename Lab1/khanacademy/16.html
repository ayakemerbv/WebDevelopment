5 min cacao cake in a microwave

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 3tbsp
Sugar 1.5tbsp
Cacao 2tbsp
Baking soda 0.5tsp
Oil 2tbsp
Milk 5tbsp

Step 1: Take all the dry ingredients and mix them in bowl.

Step 2: Add milk and mix properly

Step 3: Put it in microwave and cook for 3-5 minutes. You need to control whole process of baking because of different microwaves and different microwave powers.

Simple ice cream

  • Time: 12 hours
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Heavy Cream 500ml
Sugar 150g

Step 1: Take all the dry ingredients and mix them in bowl with mixer till its thick, stable foam.

Step 2: Put in a freezer to 5-12 hours and it's ready

Summer Lemonade

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 7
Ingredients Quantity
Sparkling water 1l.
Lemonade syrope 150ml.
Lime 1

Step 1: Cut the lime into wedges and place in a jug

Step 2: Pour in syrup and water

Step 3: Drink chilled
